Our Story

Built on Trust, Excellence & Community

Casandra's Cleaning was founded with a simple but powerful vision: to provide families and businesses in Waco with cleaning services they can trust completely. What started as a passion for creating beautiful, clean spaces has grown into Central Texas's most trusted cleaning service.

As a local, family-owned business, we understand the importance of trust when inviting someone into your home or office. That's why we've built our reputation on reliability, attention to detail, and treating every space as if it were our own.

Our commitment to excellence has earned us hundreds of 5-star reviews and long-lasting relationships with customers who have become like family. We're not just cleaning your space – we're caring for the place where your life happens.
